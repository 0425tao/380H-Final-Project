How does internet censorship work?


If we take Internet as the information highway, then accessing Internet is like driving a car on it. By the same token, the government or the ISPs functions like the toll gate. They have methods to control the Internet traffic.


General mitigation methods


  • Active subslab suction is the most common and usually the most reliable radon reduction method. One or more suction pipes are inserted through the floor slab into the crushed rock or soil underneath.

  • Sealing cracks and other openings in the foundation is a basic part of most approaches to radon reduction.

  • Enhance ventilation is another important method to reduce home radon level. You could open windows, doors and vents on the lower floors. This increase in ventilation mixes outdoor air with the indoor air containing radon, and can result in reduced radon levels.

  • Be cautious when using natural gas indoor. Because natural gas is exclusively obtained from underground sources, it may hypothesized that it contains trace amounts of radon. Increse ventilation while cooking with natrual gas would be necessary.

  • More detailed mitigation methods are in Consumer's Guide to Radon Reduction by EPA.

For high-rise building dwellers

  • High-rise buildings are immune to radon gas? No!

    Tall buildings are stacks with negative pressure compared with surrounding environment. Thus, through “stack effect”, radon gas from soil will be absorbed into the building. Besides, exposed concrete ceilings and floors release radon in the natural process of decaying stone. High-rise buildings are generally lack of ventilation, making them more subjected to radon gas.


  • Mitigation method:

    1. New building has to follow state construction code. One important method: Sub-slab depressurization. For example, not build upon underground garage.

    2. Increase air exchange.

    3. If radon radiation happens in a condo, condo board has to participate to make collective effort.
